# 1. Core Website Strategy (What the site must achieve)

From the doc, the website’s job is to:

- **Position Nuvextro** as an enterprise infrastructure consulting firm
- **Clearly explain** the unique partner-based delivery model
- **Convert visitors into leads** (primary goal)
- **Build trust** via case studies + credibility
- **Attract** both clients AND expert partners

---

## 2. Recommended Website Structure (Sitemap)

### A. Core Business Pages (High Priority)
These are non-negotiable and directly tied to your model.

#### 1. Home
- **Goal:** Positioning + conversion
- **Sections:**
    - **Hero:** “Enterprise Infrastructure & Cloud Consulting”
    - What Nuvextro does
    - Key services overview
    - How the model works (client + partner angle)
    - Featured solutions
    - Case study highlights
    - **CTA:** (Book Consultation / Contact)

#### 2. About Nuvextro
- **Goal:** Trust + clarity
- **Include:**
    - Vision & mission
    - Business model explanation (VERY important)
    - Leadership team
    - Why Nuvextro exists (problem you solve)
- **Key Strategic Point:** “We enable enterprise delivery through a network of elite engineers”

#### 3. How It Works (Important Differentiator Page)
- **Goal:** Explain your unique model
- **Split into:**
    - For Clients
    - For Partners
- **Content:**
    - Nuvextro responsibilities
    - Partner responsibilities
    - Engagement flow
    - Quality & governance model
- **Strategic Importance:** This is a key differentiator (most competitors don’t have this clarity).

#### 4. Services (Main Hub Page)
- **Goal:** Entry point to all services
- **Overview of all services**
- **Links to dedicated pages** (below)
- **CTA per service**

#### 5. Solutions (Project-Based Work)
- **Goal:** Sell high-value projects
- **Include:**
    - Cloud Migration
    - Disaster Recovery
    - Security Infrastructure
    - Network Deployment
    - Endpoint Management
- **Alignment:** This aligns with “Solution Offerings”.

#### 6. Case Studies / Portfolio
- **Goal:** Build credibility
- **3–5 strong projects** (as doc suggests)
- **Problem → Solution → Outcome format**

#### 7. Contact / Get a Quote (Critical)
- **Goal:** Lead capture
- **Include:**
    - Contact form (Formspree/Web3Forms)
    - **Fields:**
        - Name
        - Company
        - Email
        - Service needed
        - Project details
    - **Optional:** “Book a consultation”

---

### B. Service Pages (Dedicated Pages Required)
Each service from the doc should have its own page:

#### Core Services:
- Managed IT Infrastructure
- Cloud Infrastructure Management
- Network Monitoring & Management
- Cybersecurity Management
- Backup & Disaster Recovery
- IT Support Services
- These come directly from your “Initial Service Portfolio”

#### Structure of Each Service Page:
- Overview
- Problems solved
- What’s included
- Tools/technologies
- Engagement model (retainer/project)
- **CTA:** (Get Quote)

## 3. Additional High-Impact Pages (Recommended)
These are not mandatory, but strongly recommended for growth.
